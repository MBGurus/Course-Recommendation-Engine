Our Features

Personalized Recommendations

Based on student interests, academic background, and career goals, the app provides tailored subject and course suggestions that fit the student’s strengths and aspirations.

Interactive Questionnaire

The questionnaire feature engages students by gathering detailed information about their hobbies, interests, and goals, helping the system deliver more accurate recommendations.

Academic Strength Report

Students can access reports analyzing their academic performance, highlighting strong subjects, and offering insights into areas needing improvement.

Career Path Insights

The app identifies potential career paths based on selected subjects and student interests, giving users a clear picture of opportunities in different fields.

University Program Recommendations

For Grade 12 students, the app suggests university programs based on their academic performance and career interests, offering alternative pathways for broader interests.

AI-Powered Progress Tracking

The app tracks student progress, providing real-time feedback for subject selection, university programs, and career opportunities, keeping students on track.

Guidance for University Applications

Access detailed information on university programs, admission requirements, and financial aid, helping students make informed decisions when applying to university.

In-Demand Careers and Specialties

The app highlights emerging job markets and high-demand careers in South Africa, guiding students towards future-proof professions.

Future Career Vision

By focusing on the long-term impact students want to make, the app helps them shape their future career goals and vision.

User-Friendly Dashboard

The app’s intuitive dashboard, customized for students, allows easy tracking of recommendations, academic performance, and subject choices.
